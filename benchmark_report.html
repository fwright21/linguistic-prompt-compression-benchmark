Why Round 3 Appears To Work

Mechanism Observed effect
Grammar stripping Drops articles, copula, and inflection without collapsing core meaning.
Predicate-argument retention Keeps “who does what to whom,” so shorter answers still carry proposition-level content.
No framing Avoids the expansion behavior seen in the real caveman skill on explanatory questions.
No question restatement Reduces repetitive overhead, especially in comparisons and recommendations.
